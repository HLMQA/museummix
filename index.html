  • ABOUT THE PROJECT
    • This website was developed as part of my master's thesis where I am exploring digital tools. I am a student in digital humanities with a background in art history and my aim is to, one day, bring together many museum collections and cultural datasets in this interactive platform. Data visualizations and some sort of recommendation tool would be nice as well.

    Even though my programming experience is a bit limited for now, I strongly believe in what open data can bring to the cultural world. Feel free to fork my project on GitHub. Your thoughts are most welcome!
